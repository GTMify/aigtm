# Win/Loss Analyzer — Cowork Prompt

Copy and paste this into Claude Cowork. Replace the `[bracketed fields]` with your details.

---

```
I'm going to give you data from [X] deals that we [won/lost/both] in the last [timeframe].

For each deal, analyze:
1. The primary reason we won or lost (pricing, competitor, timing, product gap, champion left, no decision, or sales execution)
2. The stage where the deal was won or died
3. Any objections or competitor mentions
4. Signals we should have caught earlier

Then give me a summary covering:
- What's our #1 loss reason and how much revenue did it cost us?
- Which competitor keeps showing up and what's their pitch?
- At what stage do most deals die? (This tells us where our process breaks.)
- What do our wins have in common? (So we can do more of it.)
- 3 specific recommendations for next quarter — who should do what, and why.

## Rules
- Be direct. I want honest analysis, not consolation.
- Don't blame individual reps — focus on patterns and process.
- If the data is thin, tell me what you can't analyze and what data would help.
- If a rep's stated loss reason doesn't match the evidence, flag it diplomatically.

## Deal Data
[Paste your deal notes, CRM export, or descriptions here]
```
