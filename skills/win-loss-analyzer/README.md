# Win/Loss Analyzer Agent

Analyzes closed deals for patterns in why you win and lose, then produces actionable recommendations.

## What it does

Given deal notes, transcripts, or CRM data, this agent:
1. Categorizes each deal into win/loss reason buckets
2. Identifies the most common loss reasons by volume and revenue
3. Spots dangerous competitors and their positioning
4. Finds the stage where deals die most often
5. Produces 3-5 prioritized recommendations with clear owners

## Time saved

2-3 hours per quarterly analysis, compared to manual review and spreadsheet work.

## Customization ideas

- Add your sales stages so the agent maps to your specific pipeline
- Include your competitor list with known positioning for deeper competitive analysis
- Connect to your CRM via MCP for automated data pulls instead of copy-paste
- Add historical analysis to track whether patterns are improving or worsening quarter over quarter
