# Deal Strategy — Cowork Prompt

```
Help me build a strategy to win this deal.

## The Deal
Company: [Company Name]
Deal value: $[amount]
Stage: [Discovery / Evaluation / Proposal / Negotiation]
Expected close: [Date]
How it started: [Inbound / Outbound / Referral / Event]
What we're selling: [Product or solution]

## Who's involved
Champion (internal advocate): [Name, Title — or "none yet"]
Decision-maker (signs the contract): [Name, Title — or "unknown"]
Other stakeholders: [List anyone else involved]
Competitors: [Who else they're evaluating, or "unknown"]

## Current situation
[Describe where things stand — what's happened so far, what the next step is, any objections or concerns that have come up]

## What I need
1. **MEDDIC assessment:** Score my deal on Metrics, Economic Buyer, Decision Criteria, Decision Process, Pain, and Champion. Be honest — if I'm missing something, I need to know.
2. **Stakeholder map:** Who's in the buying committee, what do they care about, and who have I not engaged yet?
3. **Competitive positioning:** If there's a competitor, where they're strong, where we're strong, and what trap questions I can plant.
4. **Top 3 risks** and what to do about each one.
5. **Action plan:** The 3-5 most important moves to make this week, in priority order.

## Rules
- Be honest about gaps. If my deal isn't qualified, say so.
- If I only have one contact, flag that as risk #1.
- Don't give me 15 action items. Give me 3-5 that change the trajectory.
- Don't fabricate stakeholder motivations — if we don't know, say "need to discover."
```
