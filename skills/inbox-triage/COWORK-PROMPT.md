# Inbox Triage — Cowork Prompt

```
Help me triage my email backlog. I need you to categorize, prioritize, and draft responses.

## For each email, tell me:
1. **Priority bucket:**
   - 🔴 Respond Now (revenue, executives, customers, deadlines)
   - 🟡 Respond Today (important but not urgent)
   - 🟢 Respond This Week (low urgency)
   - 📌 Action Required but not an email reply (needs a meeting, doc, or decision)
   - 📁 FYI / Archive (no response needed)
   - 🗑️ Delete / Unsubscribe

2. **For 🔴 and 🟡 emails:** Draft a response under 5 sentences that answers the core ask directly.

3. **Delegation candidates:** Flag any emails someone else could handle, with a forwarding note.

4. **Patterns:** After processing everything, tell me which recurring emails could be automated, which senders could use Slack instead, and which subscriptions I should drop.

## Rules
- Match the sender's tone in your drafts.
- If you're unsure about the right answer, draft a "let me check and get back to you" response.
- Don't delete anything — just suggest.
- When in doubt, categorize as 🟡 (respond today). Silence is worse than a quick reply.
- Keep every draft under 5 sentences.

## My emails
[Paste your email content, subjects, or summaries here]
```
