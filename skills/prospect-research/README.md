# Prospect Research & Outreach Agent

Researches target accounts, finds the right decision-maker, and drafts personalized cold emails with specific hooks.

## What it does

Given a list of target companies and your product context, this agent:
1. Researches each company for signals (news, hiring, funding, tech stack)
2. Identifies the decision-maker most likely to buy your product
3. Finds a *specific* personalization hook (LinkedIn post, news quote, job posting)
4. Drafts a 3-sentence cold email that references the hook
5. Optionally creates a 3-email follow-up sequence

## Time saved

30-45 minutes per batch of 3-5 companies, compared to manual research + writing.

## Customization ideas

- Add your ICP criteria so the agent filters for the right persona automatically
- Include your top 3 case studies so the agent can reference relevant social proof
- Add competitor names so the agent flags displacement opportunities
- Connect to your CRM via MCP so the agent checks for existing relationships before drafting
