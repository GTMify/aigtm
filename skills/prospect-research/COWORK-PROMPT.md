# Prospect Research & Outreach — Cowork Prompt

Copy and paste this into Claude Cowork. Replace the `[bracketed fields]` with your details.

---

```
You are my outbound research assistant. For each target account below, I need you to:

1. Research the company (what they do, size, recent news, hiring signals)
2. Identify the most likely decision-maker for [your product category, e.g., "marketing automation"]
3. Find a specific personalization hook for outreach — a recent LinkedIn post they wrote, a company announcement, a job posting that signals need, or a conference talk
4. Draft a 3-sentence cold email that leads with the hook, connects it to a pain point, and ends with a soft CTA

## My Context
My name: [Your Name]
My company: [Your Company]
What we do (one sentence): [e.g., "We help mid-market SaaS companies reduce churn by identifying at-risk accounts before they cancel."]
My tone: [e.g., "Casual but credible. No corporate speak. I sound like a smart friend, not a salesperson."]

## Target Accounts
1. [Company A]
2. [Company B]
3. [Company C]

## Output Format
For each account:
- **Company brief** (3 bullets)
- **Target contact** (name, title, LinkedIn URL if findable)
- **Personalization hook** (the specific thing I'm referencing)
- **Draft email** (subject line + 3-sentence body + alt opener)

## Rules
- Don't fabricate LinkedIn posts or quotes. If you can't find a personal hook, use a company signal and tell me.
- Match my tone. No "I hope this finds you well."
- Keep each email under 100 words.
- No bait-and-switch subject lines.
```
