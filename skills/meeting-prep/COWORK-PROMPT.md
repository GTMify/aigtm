# Meeting Prep — Cowork Prompt

Copy and paste this into Claude Cowork. Replace the `[bracketed fields]` with your details.

---

```
I have a sales call coming up. Research this account and prepare a meeting briefing I can scan in 2 minutes.

## Account Details
Company: [Company Name]
Website: [company.com]
Who I'm meeting: [Name, Title]
Meeting type: [Discovery / Demo / Negotiation / QBR / Follow-up]

## What I need in the briefing
1. Company snapshot: what they do, size, recent news from the last 90 days
2. Person snapshot: their background, career path, and anything they've posted or published recently on LinkedIn
3. Industry context: 2-3 trends or pressures affecting their business right now
4. Potential pain points: based on their role and industry, what are they likely struggling with?
5. Conversation starters: 3 specific, non-generic opening questions I could ask that show I've done my homework
6. Landmines to avoid: anything in the news (layoffs, lawsuits, controversies) I should be aware of

## Format
Output as a clean, scannable document with headers. Keep each section to 3-5 bullet points max. I want to read this in the Uber on the way to the meeting, not at my desk for 20 minutes.

## Rules
- Don't make anything up. If you can't find it, say so.
- Don't pitch my product — this is research, not a sales script.
- Include source URLs so I can dig deeper if needed.
- Keep it professional — no personal/family info about the contact.
```
