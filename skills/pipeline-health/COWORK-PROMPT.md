# Pipeline Health Check — Cowork Prompt

```
Analyze my pipeline and give me a brutally honest health check.

## My quota
[$ amount] for [this quarter / this month]

## What I need
1. **Coverage analysis:** Do I have enough pipeline to hit my number? What's my coverage ratio?
2. **Risk flags:** Which deals have been stuck in the same stage for 30+ days? Which are single-threaded (only one contact)? Which have close dates in the past?
3. **Commit vs. upside:** Based on stage and deal health, which deals would you commit and which are upside at best?
4. **This week's top 5:** Rank the deals I should focus on this week. For each one, give me the ONE specific action to take and why it matters right now.
5. **Deals to remove:** Are there any deals I should pull from the pipeline and stop wasting time on?

## Rules
- Be a tough coach, not a cheerleader. If my pipeline is weak, say so.
- Don't sugarcoat. "This deal is dead" saves me time.
- If you don't have enough data to assess something, tell me what's missing.

## Pipeline Data
[Paste your pipeline data — CSV export, deal descriptions, or a list of deals with stage, value, and close date]
```
