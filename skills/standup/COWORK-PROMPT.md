# Morning Brief — Cowork Prompt

Copy and paste this into Claude Cowork. Replace the `[bracketed fields]` with your details and paste your data inline.

---

```
Give me a 90-second morning brief. Five sections, no preamble.

## My calendar today
[Paste meetings — time, attendees, brief description]

## Pipeline snapshot (top 5-10 deals)
[Paste: deal name, $, stage, next step, last activity]

## What happened yesterday worth knowing
[Anything notable — deals moved, customer reactions, news]

## Email subjects / Slack threads I haven't processed
[Paste subject lines or thread summaries]

## What I'm worried about (optional)
[Anything weighing on me]

## What I need
1. THE ONE THING (single highest-leverage action today)
2. Today's calendar with purpose of each meeting
3. What changed since yesterday (2-4 bullets)
4. Decisions or asks waiting on me
5. What to ignore today (protects deep work)

## Rules
- Exactly 5 sections. No preamble, no recap, no "Have a great day!"
- The one thing is one thing, not three.
- Be honest about gaps in the data I gave you.
- No hype. This is a brief, not a pep talk.
- Short beats comprehensive.
```

---

**Note:** This skill works with pasted data. For live integrations install dedicated tools (Gmail / Calendar / CRM) separately.
