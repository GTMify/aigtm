# Board / Investor Update — Cowork Prompt

```
Help me write a [monthly investor email / quarterly board update] for [Company Name].

## Key Metrics (fill in what you have)
- ARR: $[X] (plan: $[X])
- MRR: $[X]
- Net new ARR this period: $[X]
- NRR: [X]%
- Total pipeline: $[X]
- Cash: $[X] / Burn: $[X]/mo
- Headcount: [N]
- Notable wins: [Logos]
- Churn: [Logos or $]

## What happened this period
[Free text: what went well, what didn't, what changed]

## What I need
1. **TL;DR:** 3-4 sentences covering the whole story
2. **Revenue section** with actuals vs. plan and commentary
3. **Pipeline section** with coverage and velocity
4. **Customer section** with wins, churn, and health
5. **Team section** with hires, open roles, and attrition
6. **Product highlights** (keep it brief)
7. **Cash and runway** (if I gave you the data)
8. **Looking ahead** and any asks for the board

## Rules
- Every metric needs context: vs. plan, vs. prior period.
- Don't bury bad news. If we missed, say so and explain why.
- Keep it to 2 pages max for email, or outline 10 slides for a deck.
- Be precise. Boards want numbers, not narratives.
- Don't fabricate financial data I didn't give you.
```
