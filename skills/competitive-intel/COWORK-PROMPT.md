# Competitive Intel Monitor — Cowork Prompt

```
Research my top competitors and give me a weekly intel briefing.

## Competitors
1. [Competitor A — name and/or URL]
2. [Competitor B — name and/or URL]
3. [Competitor C — name and/or URL]

## For each competitor, find:
1. Product launches or feature announcements in the last 30 days
2. New job postings — what roles tell us what they're investing in
3. Press mentions, analyst coverage, or social media buzz
4. Pricing or packaging changes (check their website)
5. Key hires or departures at the leadership level
6. Customer wins they're publicizing (new logos, case studies)

## Then give me:
- A "High / Medium / Low Impact" rating for each finding
- For high-impact signals: what it means for our business and what we should do about it
- Sales talking points: for each competitor, give me a trap question, a reframe, and a proof point my team can use in deals this week

## My context
My company: [Your Company]
What we do: [One sentence]
Our main differentiator vs. these competitors: [What makes you different]

## Format
Scannable briefing I can forward to my sales team. Use signal ratings (🔴 🟡 🟢) so they can skim.
```
