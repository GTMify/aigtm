# Weekly Planner Agent

Synthesizes your calendar, pipeline, and priorities into a focused weekly game plan with day-by-day priorities and a scorecard.

## Time saved

30-45 minutes of Monday morning planning, with better outcomes because you're forced to name your priorities.

## Customization ideas

- Connect to Google Calendar via MCP to auto-pull your schedule
- Add your CRM data so pipeline priorities are based on real deal health
- Include Slack/email scan for commitments you made last week that need follow-through
- Create a Friday version that reviews the scorecard and plans the next week
