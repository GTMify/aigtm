# Weekly Planner — Cowork Prompt

```
Help me plan my week. I want a clear, prioritized game plan so I know exactly where to spend my energy.

## My week
Quota/goal this month: [$ or key metric]
What would make this a great week: [e.g., "Close the Acme deal, get 3 new demos booked, finish the QBR deck"]

## My calendar (paste or describe)
[List your meetings, calls, and commitments — or just describe what your week looks like]

## Carryover from last week
[What didn't get done that's still hanging?]

## Constraints
[PTO, travel, deadlines, things you can't move]

## What I need
1. **Priority stack:** Must do vs. should do vs. could do vs. decline/delegate
2. **Day-by-day plan:** What to focus on each day, with revenue-generating work front-loaded to early in the week
3. **Win conditions:** 3-5 specific, measurable outcomes that would make this a great week
4. **Meeting audit:** Flag any meetings that could be emails or delegated

## Rules
- Don't overschedule. Leave buffer for the unexpected.
- Front-load prospect calls and deal work to Monday-Wednesday.
- Protect at least 2 hours per day for focused work.
- If more than 60% of my time is meetings, tell me what to cut.
- Keep the plan to one page. If I won't read it, it's useless.
```
