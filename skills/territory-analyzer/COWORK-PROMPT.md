# Territory / Book of Business Analyzer — Cowork Prompt

```
Analyze my sales team's territory and give me a team-level health check.

## Team Context
Team / segment: [e.g., "Mid-Market East Coast" or "Enterprise team"]
Number of reps: [N]
Team quota this quarter: $[amount]

## What I need
1. **Rep scoreboard:** Each rep's attainment, pipeline, coverage, and status (on track / at risk / behind)
2. **Territory health:** Coverage gaps, concentration risks, overloaded or underutilized reps
3. **Whitespace:** Dormant accounts, untapped segments, expansion opportunities nobody's working
4. **Recommendations:** Specific account reallocations, coaching priorities, or hiring signals

## Rules
- Be data-driven. If the data says a rep is behind, say so — but ask for context (ramping, PTO, just closed a big deal).
- Only recommend account reallocation when the data clearly supports it. Moving accounts is disruptive.
- If I don't provide win rates or cycle lengths, skip those analyses instead of guessing.
- Keep it actionable — I need to walk away knowing what to change this quarter.

## Team Pipeline Data
[Paste your pipeline data — CRM export with rep names, accounts, deal values, stages, and close dates]
```
