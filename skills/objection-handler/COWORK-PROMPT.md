# Objection Handler / Deal Unsticker — Cowork Prompt

```
I have a deal that's stuck. Help me figure out what's happening and give me a play to get it moving.

## The Deal
Company: [Company Name]
Deal value: $[amount]
Stage: [Stage]
How long it's been stuck: [Days/weeks]

## What happened
[Describe the situation: What did they say? Who went quiet? What objection came up? What have you tried?]

## Who's involved
Champion: [Name, Title — or "lost them"]
Decision-maker: [Name, Title]
Blocker (if someone specific): [Name, Title, their concern]
Competitor: [If involved]

## What I need
1. **Diagnosis:** What's actually happening (not just what they said)
2. **The play:** A specific sequence of actions with timeline
3. **Draft messages:** Give me 3 options:
   - Option A: Address it head-on
   - Option B: Go around the blocker (different stakeholder, new angle)
   - Option C: Breakup / give them an out (often re-engages people)
4. **Escalation plan:** What if the play doesn't work?
5. **Prevention:** What should I do differently next time?

## Rules
- Don't be manipulative. No pressure tactics or fake urgency.
- If the deal is dead, tell me. I'd rather move on than waste another month.
- Messages should be under 100 words and sound human, not corporate.
- Always give me a breakup option — sometimes that's the best play.
- Respect the "no" if they've clearly said it.
```
