# QBR / Executive Business Review — Cowork Prompt

```
Help me build a QBR for an existing customer.

## Customer Details
Company: [Customer Name]
What they do: [One sentence]
Contract start: [Date]
Renewal date: [Date]
Current ARR: $[amount]

## Who's in the room
[Names and titles of attendees — this affects depth and focus]

## Data I have
[Paste whatever you have: usage metrics, support tickets, NPS scores, ROI calculations, adoption data, or just your notes on how things are going]

## What I need
1. **Executive summary:** One paragraph leading with THEIR wins, not our product
2. **Value delivered:** Metrics tied to the business outcomes they care about, with before/after
3. **Adoption & usage:** Where they're strong, where they could go deeper, benchmarks if possible
4. **Support health:** Open issues, trends, anything to address proactively
5. **What's next:** Relevant product roadmap items and recommended next steps
6. **Growth opportunity:** ONLY if the relationship is healthy — frame as solving a problem, not an upsell
7. **Renewal context:** If we're within 90 days, anchor the renewal with a value summary

## Rules
- Lead with their value, not our features.
- Use their language and their KPIs, not our product metrics.
- Don't hide bad news — address it proactively.
- Don't pitch expansion to an unhappy customer.
- If I don't have data for a section, tell me what to gather instead of making it up.
- Keep the whole thing skimmable in 5 minutes.
```
