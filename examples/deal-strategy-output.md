Marcus alone cannot authorize $180K. **Mitigation:** Ask Marcus to schedule a 20-minute "executive alignment" call with Dana by May 9. Position it as ensuring the evaluation criteria match her board reporting needs. Offer to present a CRO-specific view (not a re-demo).

2. **Unknown decision process** (Likelihood: HIGH) → You don't know if there's a security review, procurement cycle, or legal review after the evaluation. At $180K, there almost certainly is. If you discover a 6-week procurement process on June 15, this deal misses Q2. **Mitigation:** Ask Marcus this week: "After the team makes a recommendation, what are the steps before a contract gets signed? Is there a security or procurement review?" Map the full timeline backward from June 30.

3. **Clari's brand advantage** (Likelihood: MEDIUM) → If the CRO is evaluating based on analyst reports or peer recommendations, Clari's name recognition could override your operational advantages. **Mitigation:** Get your reference customer on a call with Dana. Peer validation from a CRO at a similar company neutralizes the brand gap. Schedule by May 16.
